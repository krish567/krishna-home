# SeeGroup: Multi-Layer Depth Estimation of Transparent Surfaces via Self-Determined Grouping

**Authors:** Hongyu Wen ⋅ Jia Deng

**Date/Time:** Jun 5, 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

**Location:** ExHall A & F

**Type:** POSTER

## Abstract

Transparent objects are common in daily life, and understanding their multi-layer depth information, including both the transparent surface and the objects behind it, is crucial for real-world applications that interact with transparent materials.However, existing depth methods produce only a single depth map, which is inherently ambiguous for transparent surfaces.In this work, We propose a multi-layer depth estimation method, SeeGroup, consisting of novel recurrent decomposition module design and an intensity-based formulation for multi-layer depth. Experiments demonstrate that our method significantly improves the state of the art of multi-layer depth estimation, improving quadruplet relative depth accuracy on LayeredDepth benchmark from 61.34\% to 70.67\%.
